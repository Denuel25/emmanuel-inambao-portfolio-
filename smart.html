Smart Walking for the Blind

Empowering independent mobility through smart technology

How It Works

Our system integrates cutting-edge sensors, GPS, and artificial intelligence to create a safe, independent walking experience for visually impaired individuals. Wearable devices and smartphones gather real-time data, detect obstacles, and guide users via voice instructions.

The system processes environmental data instantly and recalibrates navigation paths to provide the most efficient and secure route.

Key Features

  • Obstacle Detection: Uses advanced sensors to alert users to hazards.
  • Voice Navigation: Provides clear, step-by-step auditory guidance.
  • Real-Time Routing: Dynamically adjusts paths based on live data.
  • Accessibility First: An intuitive design optimized for ease of use.
